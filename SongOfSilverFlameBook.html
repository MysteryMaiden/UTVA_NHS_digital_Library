Song Of Silver Flame Like Night
by Amélie Wen Zhao
Book Description
In a kingdom under foreign rule, a young woman named Lan seeks to uncover the secrets of her past and the forbidden magic that could change her future. Guided by ancient myths and a mysterious stranger, she embarks on a journey that intertwines destiny, resistance, and self-discovery. Inspired by Chinese mythology, this epic fantasy weaves a tale of courage and the enduring power of one’s heritage.

Lexile Level: 870L

Publication Date:January 3, 2023

Publisher: Delacorte Press (an imprint of Random House Children’s Books)

Page Count: 480 pages
