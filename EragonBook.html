Book Description
Eragon by Christopher Paolini is the captivating story of a young farm boy whose life changes forever when he discovers a mysterious stone that turns out to be much more than it seems. Drawn into a world of dragons, magic, and ancient legends, Eragon must navigate challenges far beyond anything he’s ever known. As he learns more about his surprising destiny, he embarks on a journey that will shape the fate of an entire land. Full of adventure, mystery, and unforgettable characters, Eragon is the first book in the bestselling Inheritance Cycle—a fantasy classic that continues to inspire readers around the world.
