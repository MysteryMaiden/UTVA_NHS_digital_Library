Red Tigress
by Amélie Wen Zhao
Book Description
In this thrilling sequel to Blood Heir, Ana, the exiled princess of Cyrilia, is on the run. With the empire under the control of a dangerous usurper, Ana must navigate treacherous alliances and uncover hidden truths to reclaim her rightful place. Teaming up once again with the cunning Ramson Quicktongue, their journey takes them across perilous seas and into the heart of rebellion. As dark forces rise, Ana must harness her deadly Affinity and embrace her destiny to save her people.

Lexile Level: 850L

Publication Date:March 2, 2021

Publisher: Delacorte Press

Page Count: 448 pages

Warning: Book may contain mature topics. (best suited for readers 14+)
