About Us

The Utah Virtual Academy National Honor Society (NHS) is dedicated to recognizing and celebrating the academic achievements and leadership potential of high school students across Utah. As a chapter of the prestigious National Honor Society, we uphold the core values of Scholarship, Service, Leadership, and Character.

Our members are committed to excelling in their academic pursuits, serving their communities, and demonstrating the highest standards of integrity and responsibility. Through various service projects, leadership opportunities, and collaborative initiatives, we strive to make a positive impact both within our school and in the broader community.

As a fully online chapter, we work to connect students virtually and foster an environment of academic excellence, service, and personal growth, empowering students to thrive in their educational journeys and beyond.

Whether you're looking to challenge yourself academically, give back to the community, or become a leader among your peers, the Utah Virtual Academy NHS provides the tools and support to help you succeed.

Our Mission this Year

Our mission is to create meaningful opportunities for UTVA students to connect, grow, and succeed. Through our innovative service project, we provide safe, inclusive spaces where students can receive academic support, spark creativity, and foster connections with peers.

This year, we’re introducing exciting new resources like a chat room for friend-to-friend support, a letter-writing room to spread kindness, and a digital resource library for exploring fun and educational content. Paired with the returning favorites—tutoring, language, and gaming rooms—we aim to inspire students to reach their full potential while contributing positively to the community.

By listening to the needs of our school, we hope to make a lasting impact that goes beyond academics. Together, we’re building a brighter future, one connection at a time.

The ARL (Academic Resource Library)

The ARL is a valuable resource designed to support the academic growth and development of all UTVA students. It's a digital library filled with educational content, helpful tools, and resources created to inspire curiosity, enhance learning, and foster collaboration.

We believe that the ARL should continuously evolve based on the needs and interests of our students. That's why we encourage you to share your ideas and suggestions for new content, topics, and resources you'd like to see added. Your recommendations help us create a library that is relevant, engaging, and beneficial for everyone!

If you have any ideas for the ARL, please fill out our Content Recommendation Form to share your thoughts with us. We look forward to hearing from you!
